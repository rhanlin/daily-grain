# Quickstart: Verifying Drag Optimization

## Prerequisites
- Mobile device or Chrome DevTools (Device Mode).

## Verification Steps

### 1. Reordering Stability
1. Open "Daily Plan" page.
2. Tap and hold the **Grip Icon** (Handle).
3. Drag to reorder.
4. **Verify**: Item moves smoothly; NO Drawer opens.

### 2. Menu Access
1. Tap the **...** (More) button on the right of any card.
2. **Verify**: The "Remove from Plan" Drawer opens immediately.

### 3. Long-Press Removal
1. Long-press on the center of any task card.
2. **Verify**: No Drawer opens (previous behavior is removed).

### 4. Global Consistency
1. Go to "Category" or "Task List" views.
2. **Verify**: Long-press is removed; explicit menu buttons are present.
